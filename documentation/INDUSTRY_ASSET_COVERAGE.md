## Realistic Coverage by Platform Type

### **Small/Startup Platforms**
- **Typical Coverage**: 5,000-20,000 assets
- **Focus**: Major US stocks, popular ETFs, top cryptos
- **Examples**: New fintech apps, small trading platforms
- **Storage**: ~50-200 GB

### **Mid-Size Platforms**
- **Typical Coverage**: 20,000-100,000 assets
- **Focus**: Global equities, major indices, comprehensive crypto
- **Examples**: Most API providers, mid-tier trading platforms
- **Storage**: ~200 GB - 1 TB

### **Large Platforms**
- **Typical Coverage**: 100,000-200,000 assets
- **Focus**: Comprehensive global coverage, all asset classes
- **Examples**: Yahoo Finance, TradingView, major brokers
- **Storage**: ~1-5 TB

### **Institutional Platforms**
- **Typical Coverage**: 200,000+ assets (millions including bonds/derivatives)
- **Focus**: Everything, including alternative data
- **Examples**: Bloomberg, Refinitiv, FactSet
- **Storage**: 10+ TB
